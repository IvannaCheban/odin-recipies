Egg Fried Rice recipie

Egg Fried Rice is a Chinese recipe that you can make at home.

This rice made with simple ingredients is quick and easy. Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • some leftover rice
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 small chili pepper
  • 100 g. of canned corn
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Making process

  1. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Sauté garlic and minced chili in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Add in your corn and pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  4. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well.
  5. Season with pepper
